Project 1 — SMV Mapping Automation Bot

The Challenge

As production volumes grew, the SMV mapping process required significant manual effort — up to 30 days per cycle. There was a clear opportunity to automate this and free the team for higher-value analytical work.

The Solution

I developed a Selenium-based Python automation bot integrated with APIs that automated the entire mapping process end-to-end. The bot navigates the system, extracts data, maps values, and updates records automatically without any manual intervention.

The Impact

Mapping time reduced from 30 days to 5 days — an 83% reduction. This freed up the team to focus on higher-value analytical work and significantly improved production planning efficiency.

Project 2 — FI Date Anomaly Alert System

The Challenge

With a large volume of FI dates to track across production, there was an opportunity to build an intelligent alert system that could proactively notify the team before deadlines were missed.

The Solution

I designed a Power Automate alert system that automatically detects FI date anomalies in real time and sends instant notifications to the relevant team members, eliminating the need for manual monitoring.

The Impact

Significantly reduced manual monitoring effort, improved data accuracy, and enabled faster response times to production scheduling issues.
